History of dishes

Ramen is of Chinese origin. In Japan, this term appeared only at the end of the 19th century, when the country was experiencing an industrial revolution. The workers did not have time to cook, so they ate at ramen restaurants, as it was cheap and filling. Then students also became addicted to ramen. World War II virtually wiped out the popularity of ramen. There was a shortage of food in the country, and hunger reigned. The government has imposed severe restrictions on food supplies and profit making through restaurants.

There is no reliable information about where the word “noodles” came from, but its homeland is known for sure – China. In prehistoric times, not only rice, but also wheat was grown in huge quantities in the Celestial Empire, although it was not stored for so long. Someone came up with the idea of ​​making dough out of it and cutting it into thin strips - they were easier to pack, they were quick to prepare and could be combined with absolutely any food. At the same time, the dry product was stored for several years and remained suitable for consumption.lex

What would become modern sushi was first mentioned in China in the second century AD. They originally emerged as a way to preserve food. The fish was placed in rice and allowed to ferment, allowing a person to store edible fish for a period of time. Because fermenting foods with the right kinds of bacteria is not only safe, but can also be quite nutritious. Rice was thrown away, and fish was eaten when necessary or desired.

The history of poke begins in the deep past of the Hawaiian Islands. The word "poke" in Hawaiian means "to cut into pieces", which exactly describes the preparation of the dish. Traditionally, poke was made from fresh fish, usually tuna or salmon, cut into cubes and mixed with a variety of ingredients such as beans, pieces of vegetables, herbs, seafood, etc. This dish was popular among local fishermen.With the advent of tourism in Hawaii, poke began to attract the attention of foreigners. Featuring fresh fish and a variety of ingredients, poke quickly became a symbol of healthy and delicious food.

History of the restaurant

In 2022, we began a separate chapter in our almost 20-year gastronomic history.The brand is developing new concepts and bringing seafood cuisine to key positions in the menu, reserving the right to represent the Japanese direction as the main one.Our changes became a logical continuation of the culinary development of the company: the abundance of seafood dishes on the menu has always required a responsible attitude towards the quality and freshness of seafood. Careful and prompt transportation, preparing dishes from scratch and for each order, and not for future use, no compromises in the choice of ingredients: all these principles necessary for a seafood project have invariably been inscribed in the Ikigai ideology.
